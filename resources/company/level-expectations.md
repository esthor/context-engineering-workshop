# Engineering Level Expectations

## Level 5 - Senior Software Engineer

### Scope & Impact
- Owns and delivers projects of significant scope and complexity
- Impact spans entire team and sometimes adjacent teams
- Projects typically 2-6 months in duration
- Drives initiatives that improve team efficiency and effectiveness

### Technical Skills
- Expert in primary programming languages and technologies
- Makes sound architectural decisions for medium-large systems
- Identifies and addresses technical debt proactively
- Writes high-quality, maintainable code consistently
- Strong debugging and problem-solving skills across the stack

### Execution & Delivery
- Delivers reliably with minimal oversight
- Breaks down complex problems into manageable pieces
- Estimates accurately and meets commitments
- Manages risks and escalates appropriately
- Balances speed with quality effectively

### Collaboration & Leadership
- Mentors junior and mid-level engineers actively
- Provides thorough, educational code reviews
- Communicates technical concepts clearly to various audiences
- Collaborates effectively across teams
- Contributes to team culture and processes
- Participates in hiring and interviewing

### Business & Product Acumen
- Understands team's business context and priorities
- Asks questions about product requirements and tradeoffs
- Considers user impact in technical decisions
- Partners effectively with product managers

---

## Level 6 - Staff Software Engineer

### Scope & Impact
- Drives initiatives spanning multiple teams or critical company systems
- Impact measured across organization, not just immediate team
- Sets technical direction for significant portions of the product
- Projects and influence extend 6-12+ months

### Technical Skills
- Deep expertise in one or more technical domains
- Designs systems that scale to company needs
- Makes architectural decisions that affect multiple teams
- Identifies and drives resolution of cross-team technical issues
- Raises engineering quality bar across organization

### Execution & Delivery
- Delivers complex projects involving multiple teams
- Navigates ambiguity and uncertainty effectively
- Drives projects to completion despite obstacles
- Creates leverage through tools, frameworks, and patterns
- Balances strategic and tactical work appropriately

### Collaboration & Leadership
- Influences technical direction beyond immediate team
- Mentors senior engineers and helps develop future leaders
- Builds consensus across teams and stakeholders
- Represents engineering in cross-functional planning
- Improves engineering culture and practices company-wide
- Key participant in hiring and raising the hiring bar

### Business & Product Acumen
- Deeply understands business model and company strategy
- Proactively identifies opportunities for technical solutions to business problems
- Partners with leadership on product/technical strategy
- Evaluates technical investments through business lens
- Influences roadmaps across multiple teams

---

## Transition from L5 to L6

Common growth areas for Senior Engineers aspiring to Staff:

1. **Scope Expansion**: Move from owning projects to owning outcomes across multiple teams
2. **Strategic Thinking**: Shift from executing on roadmap to shaping roadmap
3. **Influence**: Develop ability to drive change without direct authority
4. **Communication**: Increase visibility and impact through writing and presentations
5. **Navigating Ambiguity**: Get comfortable with less-defined problems and situations
6. **Business Acumen**: Deepen understanding of company business and strategy

Typical timeline: 12-24 months of sustained performance demonstrating Staff-level scope and impact.
