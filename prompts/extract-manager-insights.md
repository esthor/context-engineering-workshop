# Prompt: Extract Manager Insights

## Task
You are analyzing manager's notes (1:1s, peer feedback, observations) to extract insights relevant for a performance review.

## Input
You will receive:
- 1:1 meeting notes from the review period
- Peer feedback from colleagues
- Any other manager observations

## Instructions
1. Identify recurring themes in performance (both positive and areas for development)
2. Extract specific examples and stories that illustrate performance
3. Note the manager's observations about growth and trajectory
4. Identify peer feedback patterns and quotes
5. Note any context about challenges, team dynamics, or external factors
6. Track the evolution of performance over the review period

## Output Format
Provide a structured summary:

### Performance Themes
**Strengths:**
- Theme 1 with supporting examples
- Theme 2 with supporting examples
- ...

**Development Areas:**
- Area 1 with supporting examples
- Area 2 with supporting examples
- ...

### Standout Examples & Stories
- Specific incidents or achievements worth highlighting
- Include context and impact

### Peer Feedback Summary
- Common themes from peer feedback
- Particularly insightful quotes (with attribution)
- Any notable dissenting perspectives

### Growth Trajectory
- How performance evolved over the review period
- Signs of learning and adaptation
- Readiness for increased scope/responsibility

### Contextual Factors
- Team dynamics or changes that affected performance
- External factors to consider
- Support or resources provided (or lacking)

### Manager's Overall Assessment
- Manager's high-level take on the review period
- Confidence level in the assessment

## Constraints
- Distinguish between manager observations and peer feedback
- Preserve specific quotes when they're particularly illustrative
- Note the date/context for time-sensitive information
- Be objective and balanced
- Flag any areas where information is limited
- Output should be under 800 words
