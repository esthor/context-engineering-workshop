# Prompt: Extract Performance Review Framework

## Task
You are analyzing company performance review guidelines to extract the core framework, evaluation criteria, and standards that should be applied when writing performance reviews.

## Input
You will receive company training materials and guidelines about performance reviews.

## Instructions
1. Identify the evaluation dimensions (categories of performance being assessed)
2. Extract the rating scale and what each rating means
3. Note key criteria for each evaluation dimension
4. Identify any specific guidelines for writing effective reviews
5. Extract examples of good practices and things to avoid

## Output Format
Provide a structured summary in the following format:

### Evaluation Dimensions
- Dimension 1: [name and brief description]
- Dimension 2: [name and brief description]
- ...

### Rating Scale
- Rating 1: [name and criteria]
- Rating 2: [name and criteria]
- ...

### Writing Guidelines
- Key guideline 1
- Key guideline 2
- ...

### Important Notes
- Any other critical information for review writers

## Constraints
- Focus on the framework and standards, not the process/timeline
- Be concise and actionable
- Preserve specific terminology used in the source materials
- Output should be under 500 words
